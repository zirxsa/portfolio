Solo · Final Year Project 2026 – Present

CoreBlaster

Head-tracked cycling exergame with AI fitness analytics

A real-time exergame where players pedal a stationary bike to fire lasers and use webcam head-tracking (MediaPipe) to aim, integrating live Bluetooth cadence and heart-rate sensors into Unity over a custom Python↔Unity UDP bridge. A Python ML pipeline grades each session’s performance and estimates fitness metrics (VO₂max via ACSM/Åstrand/Tanaka, calories via Keytel), surfaced in an in-game analytics dashboard with charts, strengths/weaknesses, and personalized coaching tips.

Collected 32 real workout sessions from 14 players and ran a model comparison (Random Forest vs SVM vs XGBoost) with stratified k-fold cross-validation, achieving a 0.94 F1 score for player-performance classification — average cadence emerged as the top predictive feature.
